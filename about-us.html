Our History




Shenzhen Cosmos Dental Technology was established in 2010. Our company is a professional large-scale manufacturer of all kinds of high quality denture, integrating development and production together.


Our company has nearly 150 experienced employees, 1800 square meters of production area and introduced a number of high-end professional production equipment, which has developed rapidly. The company is located in Shenzhen city strip, near the baoan airport in Shenzhen, close to subway stationand shekou port.


We enjoy convenient water, land and air transportation. Our products are superior in quality and reasonable price in the market. We are also well-known for the best service and on time delivery.

Communication, understanding, concentration and teamwork spirit are the signs of our corporate culture.



Our Factory


The company has a 10 to 15 years skillful and experienced technical team, strong technical capability, clean and orderly working environment, human nature and institutionalization of combining the management mode, to provide high quality products for the guidelines, by the good faith management idea, advanced equipment, exquisite craftsmanship, good technical team and excellent after-sales service in the customer base to form a good reputation.



Our Product


The main products of company include: Porcelain crown, All porcelain crown, Full metal crown, Valplast, Acrylic, Full dental, Framework, Retainer, Implant, Diagnostic and so on.



Product Application


The products are mainly used in medical care, functional repair and aesthetic effects.



Our Certificate


Our company is a state-approved medical device manufacturer with the "Medical Device Production License" and "Product Registration Certificate" issued by Guangdong drug administration. We have attained the FDA certification, CE certification, ISO 13485 and 9001 certification.
